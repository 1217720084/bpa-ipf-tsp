How to change the screen resolution:

1) Be logged in as "root" or "su"

2) Type "set800" to change resolution to SVGA 800x600.

3) Type "set1024" to change resolution to Virtual-XGA 1024x768.

4) Exit from the X-windows desktop, and restart X-windows with 
   the command "startx". The X-windows desktop with restart in 
   the newly-set resolution mode.
